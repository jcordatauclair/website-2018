ABOUT

      My name is Julien. I am a 21-year-old amateur photographer and engineering student based in France. My passion for photography began almost 10 years ago when I was taking pictures of birds in my garden. Today, my attraction for the outside world is still present and through my images, I try to transmit emotions and share my vision of the world. My dream would be to be able to live from this passion that continues to grow over the years. If you have any question, request or if you want to discuss, please do not hesitate to contact me. I'd love to hear from you!


— Julien
